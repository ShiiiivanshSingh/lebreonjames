Kobe and Basketball Memes

The Kobe Meme

The "Kobe!" meme is popular among basketball fans. It refers to the action of shouting "Kobe!" when throwing something, mimicking Kobe Bryant's legendary shooting skills.

Other Basketball Memes

Missing the hoop completely can be embarrassing, but it's a part of the game. The "Airball" meme highlights these funny moments.
